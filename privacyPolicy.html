General rules

Personal information is information about a living individual, and information that can identify the individual by the name and resident registrati
on number contained in the information (even if the information alone cannot identify a specific individual, it can be easily combined with other in
formation includes those that can be identified). Seoul Garden Hotel attaches great importance to the protection of customers' personal information
and complies with the personal information protection regulations under the 「Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network
Utilization and Information Protection, etc.」 and 「Personal Information Protection Guidelines」 established by the Ministry of Public Admini
stration and Security.

Through the privacy policy, Seoul Garden Hotel informs you about the purpose and method of using the personal information provided by custom
ers and what measures are being taken to protect personal information.

Seoul Garden Hotel discloses the privacy policy on the first screen of its website so that customers can check it easily at any time.

Seoul Garden Hotel establishes the necessary procedures to revise the personal information handling policy for continuous improvement of the per
sonal information handling policy. In addition, when revising the privacy policy, a version number, etc. is given so that customers can easily recogn
ize the revised information.

    Seoul Garden Hotel prepares a procedure for the customer to click the “I agree” button or “I do not agree” button to the contents of the Seoul Garden Hotel’s privacy

  3. 3. COLLECTION AND REJECTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION BY COOKIES

    Seoul Garden Hotel uses cookies to store and retrieve customer information for some services. Cookies are small pieces of information that a website server sends to
    your computer's browser. After accessing the Seoul Garden Hotel website, the customer must allow cookies to use the online reservation service. You have a choice
    about cookies and can choose whether to use them. You can accept all cookies, check each time a cookie is saved, or refuse to save all cookies. However, if you refuse to
    store cookies, it may interfere with the use of some services. Cookies identify your computer, but do not personally identify you, and expire when the computer
    browser is closed.

    Example of setting method (in case of Internet Explorer): Tools at the top of the web browser > Internet Options > Personal Information
  4. 4. NON-PURPOSE USE AND PROVISION AND SHARING TO THIRD PARTIES

    • 1. Seoul Garden Hotel uses customers' personal information within the scope notified in 『Purpose of Collection and Use of Personal Information』, and does not
      use it beyond this range or provide it to others or other companies/organizations. In particular, We will use and provide personal information with care.

      1. -Affiliate relationship

        In order to provide better service, we may provide or share customers' personal information with affiliates. However, the name of the affiliate, the purpose of the
        partnership, the service provided, the scope of sharing of personal information, the purpose of use, and the period of the partnership will be notified in detail in ad
        vance, and consent will be sought through e-mail or in writing.

      2. -Consignment processing

        In the case of consignment processing of user's personal information for smooth business processing, the name of the consigned processing company, the scope of
        the consigned personal information, the consignment purpose, the consignment processing process, and the consignment period shall be notified in detail in
        advance.

    • 2. Notification and consent method Through the online website, at least 10 days in advance, and at the same time, individually through e-mail, etc. at least once.
    • 3. Exceptions
      • -When there is a request from a related institution for investigative purposes in accordance with related laws and regulations - When
        providing to an advertiser's partner or research group in a form that cannot identify a specific individual for statistical preparation, academic research, or market
        research         -Other related laws If there is a request according to the procedure set out in However, even in exceptional cases, when information is provided according to related laws or at the request of an investigation agency, it is in principle to notify the
        person concerned. We may not be able to give notice due to legal reasons. We will do our best not to provide information indiscriminately against the original
        purpose of collection and use.
  5. 5. READING AND CORRECTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

    The customer can view or correct the registered customer's personal information at any time. If you contact the person in charge of personal information manage
    ment and the person in charge in writing, by phone or by e-mail, we will take action within the minimum time set according to the business procedure.
    If a customer requests correction of errors in personal information, the personal information will not be used or provided until the correction is completed.
    If incorrect personal information has already been provided to a third party, we will notify the third party of the result of the correction without delay and take
    measures to correct it.

  6. 6. WITHDRAWAL OF CONSENT TO COLLECTION, USE AND PROVISION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

    Customers can withdraw their consent to the collection, use and provision of personal information through online reservations at any time. If you contact the person
    in charge of personal information management and the person in charge in writing, by phone, or by e-mail, we will immediately take necessary measures such as
    deletion of personal information. In the event of withdrawal of consent and the destruction of personal information, we will notify the customer without delay.

  7. 7. RETENTION PERIOD AND DESTRUCTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION
    • 1. Customer's personal information will be destroyed when the purpose of collecting or providing personal information is achieved as follows. In case of withdrawal
      from membership or expulsion from membership, the purpose of retention, period, and items of personal information to be retained are specified in advance to
      obtain consent.
      1. - Records that need to be preserved as evidence in litigation or dispute : 5 years
      2. - Records on consumer complaints or dispute resolution : 3 years
      3. If a customer requests to view transaction information, etc. held with the customer's consent, Seoul Garden Hotel will take measures so that the reading can be
        confirmed without delay.
    • 2.Destruction method
      Seoul Garden Hotel immediately destroys the personal information after the 'purpose of personal information processing' is achieved or the 'processing and
      retention period' has elapsed.
      1. - Personal information printed on paper is destroyed by shredding or incineration
      2. - Personal information stored in the form of an electronic file is deleted using a technical method that cannot reproduce the record.
  8. 8. TECHINICAL AND ADMINISTRATIVE MEASURES FOR PERSONAL INFORMATION PROTECTION
    1. 1. Technical measures
      Seoul Garden Hotel takes the following technical measures to ensure safety in handling customers' personal information so that personal information is not lost,
      stolen, leaked, altered or damaged.
      • - Customer's personal information is protected by password.
      • - Seoul Garden Hotel is taking measures to prevent damage caused by computer viruses by using a vaccine program. Vaccine programs are updated periodically, and
        in the event of a sudden virus outbreak, the vaccine is provided as soon as it is released to prevent infringement of personal information.
      • - In order to prepare for unauthorized external intrusion, we use firewalls, etc. to ensure security.
    2. 2. Administrative measures
      • - Seoul Garden Hotel limits access to customers' personal information to a minimum number of people. Those who fall under the minimum number of people are as
        follows.
        1. 1. Persons who perform marketing tasks directly with users
        2. 2. Persons who perform personal information management tasks such as the person in charge of personal information management and the person in charge
        3. 3. Persons whose handling of personal information is unavoidable for other business
        purposes - For employees who handle personal information As a result, regular in-house training and outsourced training are conducted on acquisition of new security technologies and obligations to protect personal information.
      • - Internal procedures are in place to prevent information leakage by humans through the security pledge of the personal information handler at the time of joining the company, and to audit the implementation of the personal information handling policy and the compliance of the employees.
      • - The handover of personal information-related handlers is carried out thoroughly with security maintained, and responsibility for personal information accidents after joining and leaving the company is clarified. Access is controlled by setting the computer room and data storage room as special protection areas.
      • - In the event that personal information is lost, leaked, altered, or damaged due to the mistake of an internal manager or an accident in technical management, Seoul Garden Hotel will immediately notify you and take appropriate measures.

    2. 2. When the customer consents to the transmission of e-mails such as newsletters, Seoul Garden Hotel takes measures such as the following in the subject and body

    3. 3. When the Seoul Garden Hotel transmits advertising information by e-mail, etc. for online marketing such as product information guidance, the subject and body
      of the e-mail shall contain the following measures so that customers can easily recognize it.
      • - Subject line of E-mail
        Displays the phrase (advertisement) in Korean without spaces at the beginning of the subject line, followed by the main content of the e-mail body line.
      • - The body of the E-mail
        Specify the name, e-mail address, and phone number of the sender to which the customer can express his/her intention to reject the reception. The method by
        which the customer can easily express his/her intention of refusal of reception is specified in Korean and English respectively.
      • - In the case of transmitting commercial advertising information through text transmission other than e-mail, such as fax or mobile phone text transmission,
        the text (advertising) shall be written at the beginning of the transmission and the sender's contact information shall be specified in the transmission.
